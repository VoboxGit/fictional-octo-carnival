403 Forbidden

A "403" error code, also known as "Forbidden," indicates that a web server understands your request but is refusing to authorize access to the requested resource, meaning you don't have permission to view the page or perform the action due to insufficient permissions on the server side; essentially, you are not allowed to access that specific content.
