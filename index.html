About Us

Born from a side hustle, built with purpose.

In 2005, Scott Westman started Vobox as a passion project—a quiet pursuit of something bigger. For years, it remained in the background, growing steadily until the time and place felt right.

That time is now.

At Vobox, we believe in cutting through the clutter. Our mission is to help people unlock their potential without getting tangled in red tape. We’re here to simplify the complex, empower the bold, and make progress feel possible.

Vobox isn’t just a name—it’s a mindset. And we’re just getting started.
